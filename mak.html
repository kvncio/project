Makkah

Mecca is the holiest city for Muslims. It contains the Grand Mosque and the Kaaba 
which is the qibla for Muslims in their prayers.
It is located in the west of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, about 400 kilometers from Medina in the southwestern direction, and about 75 kilometers from the city of Taif in the western direction, and 72 kilometers from the city of Jeddah and the Red Sea coast, 
and its closest port is the Jeddah Islamic Port.
